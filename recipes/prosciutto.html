Prosciutto-Covered Toasted Cheese Sandwiches

Item Description:

Fresh Prosciutto imported from New Zealand, Swiss Cheese flown from Taiwan, this dish's simplicity and remarkably uncomplicated taste will satiate any human or non-humans taste buds on a Thursday Afternoon. Best if enjoyed with a sparkling glass of rice horchata.

Ingredient List

  • Dry-cured Prosciutto
  • Sliced Sourdough Bread
  • Mayo and House-Chili Sauce Blend
  • Thin Swiss Cheese Slices
  • Fresh Basil Strips
